Case study

PIAGA is a specialized provider of coating and painting materials, covering several industries, such as aerospace, automotive, industrial and construction.

PIAGA manages a catalog of more than 20 paint and accessories brands. This is why we saw the need to design a catalog that includes all the products they offer to their customers.

In this editorial design project, we managed to structure every content, displaying over 3,000 products in an attractive and friendly way.

Part of the graphic result was also applied on a web site to complement this product catalog and corporate business information.

Services

Content and editorial design, product photography (by Fernando Vega) and web design and copywriting.
